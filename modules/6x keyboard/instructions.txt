Instructions:

Print the model
cut wire: 
- 6 wires to cover row 1 to the sockets
- 6 wires to cover row 2 to the sockets
strip wire ends
pre-solder sockets (top and bottom pin)
pre-solder wires
pre-solder pins on keyboard switches
solder wires to keyboard switches
thread wires trough holes in the print to where the sockets go (alternate a long one (second row) first, then a short one (first row))
solder wire to socket and tighten
repeat for all switches
test
place keycaps
place a strip of hot glue on top of switches and secure cover plate
glue velcro strips to bottom


done

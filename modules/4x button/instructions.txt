
Instructions:

Pre-solder the button terminals
Pre-solder the socket terminals and bend the ground pin down and the middle pin inwards.
measure and cut 8 wires to length, some extra is no problem.
strip wires
pre-solder wires (both ends) and solder them to the buttons
slide in buttons and makre sure wire is long enough
press down button (tight fit) (if button placement is wrong, you can press the buttons up from holes on the bottom of the 3D print)
solder wires to sockets
test (if possible)
close print
hot glue strips of velcro.

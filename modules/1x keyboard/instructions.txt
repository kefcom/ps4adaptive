Instructions:

Print the model
cut and strip 2 wires
pre-solder socket (top and bottom pin)
pre-solder wires
pre-solder pins on keyboard switch
solder wires to keyboard switch
thread wires trough holes in the print to where the socket goes
solder wire to socket and tighten
test
place keycaps
place a strip of hot glue on top of socket and on the side of the cover plate, then secure cover plate
glue velcro strip to bottom


done

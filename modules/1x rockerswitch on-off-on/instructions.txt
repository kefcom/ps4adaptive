instructions:

strip wires
pre-solder wires
pre-solder rocker switch
pre-solder sockets

solder wires to rocker switch (2 wires to the middle terminal, one to the upper and bottom one)
place switch and thread wires (1 from the middle + 1 from the top or bottom) to each side
solder wires to the sockets (the wire from the middle goes to the ground pin (the one on the outside))
place sockets and tighten

place cover.

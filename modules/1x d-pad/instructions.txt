instructions:

cut button pins to length (about half of the original size)
pre-solder button pins
pre-solder wires
pre-solder sockets

solder wires to buttons
thread wires as shown in the video and place buttons

solder wires to sockets

place sockets

test (if possible)

place coverplate

place flexpad on the buttons
place d-pad rocker and topcover

if it all fits nice, glue the topcover in the corners and stick it on the design.

glue velcro strips
